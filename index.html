Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who are the tutors on your platform?

Our tutors are highly qualified professionals with expertise in various subjects. They undergo a rigorous selection process and background checks to ensure quality.

2. How do I find a tutor that suits my needs?

Based on your requirement, our team will handpick the best tutor for you. Demo session will be provided by the tutor and if you are not satisfied, we will allocate another tutor. Our team will monitor the classes to ensure quality.

3. How are the classes conducted?

Classes are conducted using various online meeting platforms. Our team will guide you on how to use the platform.

4. Can I choose the frequency and schedule of my lessons?

We will schedule the classes based on your convenient timing.

5. Do you offer group tutoring sessions?

Yes, we offer both one-on-one and group tutoring sessions, depending on your preference and learning needs.
